Whether car, bicycle or public transport - we need new ideas to enable good and barrier-free mobility for everyone.

🖈

Those who rely on the car should get through the city more quickly.

I am committed to neighborhood garages where residents, visitors and traders can park.

🖈

Cycling in Leipzig must be safer!

I want to ensure that there are more bicycle roads that are not parked and that there is as little traffic as possible.

🖈

Driving bans do not help, but more public transport offers with additional routes and a higher frequency.

I want to relieve frequent drivers and a simple payment model that takes into account actually driven routes.
